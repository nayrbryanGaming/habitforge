# HabitForge App Store Screenshot Guide

To maximize conversion on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store, your screenshots must tell a story of transformation. Follow this guide to capture and design your marketing assets.

## Screenshot Order & Narrative

### 1. The Hero Dashboard (Value Prop)
- **What to show**: The main dashboard with 3-4 active habits and a "50% Completion" progress bar.
- **Headline**: "Forge Better Habits Every Day."
- **Focus**: The clean, glassmorphic UI.

### 2. Habit Logging (Simplicity)
- **What to show**: A user tapping a checkbox with the "Celebrate" Lottie animation mid-air.
- **Headline**: "Minimal Input. Maximum Feedback."
- **Focus**: The ease of use and haptic satisfaction.

### 3. Analytics (Insights)
- **What to show**: Individual habit performance with the BarChart and "90% Success Rate" stat.
- **Headline**: "Master Your Momentum."
- **Focus**: Data-driven self-improvement.

### 4. Streak System (Motivation)
- **What to show**: A glowing fire icon next to a 30-day streak.
- **Headline**: "Never Break the Chain."
- **Focus**: Streak psychology and retention.

### 5. Premium Storefront (Social Proof)
- **What to show**: The Premium screen with "PRO" features highlighted.
- **Headline**: "Unlock Your Full Potential."
- **Focus**: Unlimited habits and deep AI insights.

## Technical Specifications

### Google Play Store
- **Phone**: 1080 x 1920 or 1440 x 2560 (16:9)
- **Tablet**: 2048 x 1536 (4:3)
- **File Format**: PNG or high-quality JPG.

### App Store (iOS)
- **6.5" Display**: 1282 x 2778
- **5.5" Display**: 1242 x 2208
- **iPad Pro**: 2048 x 2732

---

## Design Tips
*   **Device Frames**: Use high-fidelity iPhone 15 or Pixel 8 frames (available in Figma).
*   **Backgrounds**: Use soft gradients from the HabitForge palette (`#2563EB` to `#F8FAFC`).
*   **Typography**: Use **Inter** or **Outfit** for headlines.
*   **Clarity**: Ensure text is legible on small device screens.
