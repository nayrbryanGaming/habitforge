# HabitForge Product Strategy

## 🎯 Target Market
- **Students & Academics**: Focusing on study routines and health.
- **Remote Workers**: Managing work-life balance and focus.
- **Productivity Enthusiasts**: Users who value data-driven self-improvement.

## 💎 Value Proposition
"Forge unbreakable habits through behavioral psychology and precision tracking."

## 🚀 Growth Strategy
1. **Viral Loops**: Streak sharing on social media.
2. **Community**: Future integration of "Challenges" to drive retention.
3. **Analytics**: Providing deeper insights than competitors to create "Lock-in" value.

## 💰 Monetization
- **Freemium Model**: 5 Habits free.
- **Pro Subscription**: Unlimited habits, AI insights, and priority reminders for $4.99/mo.
