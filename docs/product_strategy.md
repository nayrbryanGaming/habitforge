# Product Strategy & Growth

## The Core Loop
1. **Trigger:** Push notification reminder (Smart localized time).
2. **Action:** One-tap completion in the HabitForge app.
3. **Reward:** Visual streak fire animation + progress bar filling up.
4. **Investment:** Reviewing weekly analytics, creating more habits.

## Growth Strategy
**Initial Target:** 10,000 installs in 3 months.
**Retention Goal:** 30% monthly active users (MAU).

### Acquisition Channels
1. **Product Hunt Launch:** Positioned as the "Minimalist Habit Tracker for Founders".
2. **Reddit Communties:** Grassroots posting in r/productivity, r/getdisciplined, r/selfimprovement.
3. **TikTok Content:** "Day in the life" aesthetics showing the app bridging routine gaps.

### Retention Mechanics
Habit trackers live and die on Day 7 and Day 30 retention.
- **Streaks:** Utilizing "loss aversion." Humans hate breaking a streak more than they enjoy starting one.
- **Smart Reminders:** Contextual reminders prevent passive churn.

## Monetization Model
**Freemium.** 
Why not paid upfront? Habit tracking requires a low barrier to entry. Free users acting as DAU improve app store rankings.
- **Free:** Limit of 5 habits. Basic tracking.
- **Pro ($4.99/mo):** Unlimited habits, deep analytical insights, AI personalized scheduling recommendations.
