# Data Usage Policy

To maintain transparency regarding how HabitForge handles your data:

## 1. What data is collected?
- Email addressing, Profile Name (Authentication)
- Habit definitions (Title, Schedule, Icon, Color)
- Habit logs (Timestamps of completion)

## 2. Who has access?
- **You:** Full access to your own data via the HabitForge app.
- **Our Systems:** Automated functions analyze data to generate streaks and reminder times.
- **Third Parties:** We DO NOT share or sell your individual habit data to advertising networks. 

## 3. Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to map aggregated user trends (e.g., "What percentage of users complete onboarding?") to improve app UX. This data is strictly anonymized.

## 4. Deletion
You retain complete ownership over your data. A hard deletion triggered from the profile view eradicates your UID from our Firebase Auth and Firestore tables permanently.
