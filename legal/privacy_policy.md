# Privacy Policy for HabitForge

**Last Updated: April 11, 2026**

At HabitForge, accessible from the Google Play Store, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by HabitForge and how we use it.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

### Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:
- Email address
- First name and last name
- Usage Data (e.g., how you interact with habit logs)

## 2. Use of Data
HabitForge uses the collected data for various purposes:
- To provide and maintain our Service
- To notify you about changes to our Service
- To provide customer support
- To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service
- To monitor the usage of our Service
- To detect, prevent and address technical issues

## 3. Storage and Security of Data
Your data is stored securely using Firebase (a Google Cloud service). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

## 4. Your Data Protection Rights
Under certain conditions, you have the following data protection rights:
- **The right to access, update or delete** the information we have on you.
- **The right of rectification.** You have the right to have your information rectified if that information is inaccurate or incomplete.
- **The right to object.** You have the right to object to our processing of your Personal Data.

## 5. Account Deletion & Data Retention [CRITICAL]
HabitForge provides a clear and accessible way for users to delete their account and all associated data.
1. **In-App**: Navigate to **Settings** and tap on **"Delete Account"**.
2. **Web-Based Request**: You can request account deletion by submitting the form at **https://habitforge.app/#data-safety** or by contacting our support email directly.
3. Upon confirmation, the following data is **permanently and immediately** deleted from our production databases:
   - Your user profile and account credentials
   - All created habits and routine configurations
   - Complete history of habit logs and streaks
   - All performance analytics and mastery data

We do not retain backup copies of your personal data after account deletion.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

## 7. Service Providers
We use trusted third-party services to ensure the best performance:
- **Firebase**: Used for real-time database, authentication, and push notifications.
- **Google Analytics for Firebase**: Used to understand app usage and improve UX.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

- Via our website: https://habitforge.app/contact
