Data Usage Policy

GOOGLE PLAY COMPLIANT

1. Transparent Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand app usage patterns. The following data is processed in an anonymized format:

  • Frequency of habit completion.
  • Feature engagement (e.g., how often the Forge Heatmap is viewed).
  • Total time spent within the HabitForge architecture.

2. Push Notification Token Management

HabitForge collects and stores your FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) Device Token. This token is used strictly to deliver your scheduled habit reminders. Tokens are refreshed periodically and can be revoked by the user by disabling notifications in system settings.

3. Third-Party Services

All user data is hosted within Google Cloud Platform (Firebase). We do not integrate with third-party data brokers or advertisement networks. Your data remains isolated within the HabitForge ecosystem.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as the account remains active. Upon "Atomic Purge" (Account Deletion), all database records are scrubbed within 24 hours via an automated Cloud Function trigger.
