Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

HabitForge ("we," "our," or "us") provides a habit-tracking platform designed to help users build consistent routines through behavioral science. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Google Play Developer Distribution Agreement and User Data policies.

2. Data Collection

Account Information: We collect your email and name via Google Firebase Authentication to enable cross-device synchronization.

Habit Data: We store habit titles, schedules, and completion logs in encrypted Cloud Firestore databases.

Device Information: We collect anonymized device identifiers for push notifications and analytics via Firebase Cloud Messaging and Google Analytics for Firebase.

3. Data Usage

We use your data strictly to provide core app features, including:

  • Calculating streaks and progress analytics.
  • Sending scheduled habit reminders.
  • Improving app performance through anonymous usage statistics.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Deletion & User Rights

In compliance with modern privacy standards (GDPR/CCPA/Google Play Policies):

Right to Erasure: You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app settings. This "Atomic Purge" permanently removes your profile, habits, logs, and analytics from our servers.

5. Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS encryption in transit. We leverage Google Cloud's secure infrastructure to prevent unauthorized access.
